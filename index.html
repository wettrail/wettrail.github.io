Summer rain showers ever leave seaching your favorite radar feed to see which of you local trail got dumped on? Planning a weekend trip to ride some trail outside your home regions and don't if it has been soaked recently. This web page is designed to anwser these questions quickly.

This site has a curated list of the top mountain biking locations in each state. Using percise GIS locations and realtime Radar data from NOAA easily searchable lists are create with rain total for the last 12, 24, 48 and 72 hours. Allowing you to easily dry trails in your region.

Use the state lookup page to navigation to your state of interest. Then use <Ctrl>+f to locate your trail by name.

Don't see your trail listed? Use the request tab link the a google form to request to have a trial added.

Book mark your favorite page and take a quick look when you are prepping for your next ride. The site updates every hour so you will always have the latest information.
