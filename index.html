Summer rain showers ever get ruin a ride? Planning a weekend trip to ride some trail outside your home regions and don't if they have been soaked recently. This web page is designed to anwser these questions quickly.

This site has a curated list of the top mountain biking location in each state. Using percise GIS locations real time Radar data from NOAA is used to estimate rain fall amounts for the trails. Or better yet which trails are dry and ready to ride.

Use the state lookup page to navigation to your state of interest. Then use +f to find locat your trail by name.

Don't see your trail listed? Use the request tab link the a google form to request to have a trial added.

Book mark your favorite page and take a quick look when you are prepping for your next ride. The site updates every hour so you will always have the latest information.
